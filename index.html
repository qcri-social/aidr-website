AIDR —Artificial Intelligence for Disaster Response— is an easy-to-use, free and open-source platform that filters and classifies microblog messages broadcast during humanitarian crises. AIDR uses human and machine intelligence to collect and automatically tag up to thousands of messages per minute. Learn more »
